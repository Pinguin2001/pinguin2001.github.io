Privacy policy for Bluebird webbrowser

This privacy policy outlines how personal information is collected and used by Bluebird.
By using Bluebird Browser you agree to this privacy policy.

Bluebird itself does not collect any data, however since we rely on third party technology such as WinUI and WebView2, data might be collected by them.
We suggest to read the privacy policy of these services.
You can obtain a full list of all third party services used in the attributions section of the about page.

We can modify or update this privacy policy at any time without notifying the user.
Users should review this privacy policy periodically.
