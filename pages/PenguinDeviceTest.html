PenguinDeviceTest
Device test app mainly intended for Lumia phones

Download (v1.0)

Download certificate

Features:

- Test your screen for dead pixel/burn-in
- Read battery info such as capacity, charge rate and fully-charged energy capacity
- Test vibration motor
- Test speaker
- Test/read sensor data (currently Accelerometer, Barometer, Light sensor and Magnetometer)
- Test camera button/usb keyboard
